An offset-mirror with Red Cloak in the tilted dualistic way most of Miko is. The inversion effects on many of the Administrix's cards serve two purposes. First off, they provide a decent duality-invoking visual gimmick and differentiation for material derived from the base game's card art. Secondly, it highlights cards that themselves are noticeably dualistic- either they have self-debuffs attached to buffs, or they indicate cards that reflect both Yin and Yang (directly or through Affinity). This second point is broken for this card in particular, simply because an earlier version provided Affinity and the art / concept was too nice to cut otherwise.
