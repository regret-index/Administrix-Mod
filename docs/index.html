Keywords

Keyword Description
Plot Plot effects trigger on both drawing and manually discarding given cards.
Transpose To Transpose is to discard cards from your Draw Pile, then move different cards from the Discard Pile to the bottom of your Draw Pile.
Immortal Immortal cards upgrade other Immortal cards via Plot and can be upgraded any number of times.
[Artifact] Negate debuffs as usual, including the loss of stats from temporary stat increases.
Wilting Whenever you play a card, Wilting does 1 damage to yourself.
Yin Yang fuels many cards. If you have more Yin than Yang, then when you gain Yin, damage all enemies twice.
Yang Yin fuels many cards. If you have more Yang than Yin, then when you gain Yang, gain Block.
Daybreak Daybreaks are 0-cost Ethereal attacks that give Yang and Exhaust.
Nightfall Nightfalls are 0-cost Ethereal skills that give Yin and Exhaust.
Affinity Increases the effectiveness of Yin and Yang.
